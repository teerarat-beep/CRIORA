Why CRIORA Matters

As populations age, consumers seek protein sources that are nutritious, digestible, functional, and environmentally sustainable. CRIORA bridges this gap.

Health & Active Aging

Supports the development of functional protein products tailored for active aging populations, providing easy-to-digest nutrition without bitterness.

Sustainability

Utilizes cricket protein, a highly resource-efficient and environmentally sustainable alternative protein source compared to traditional livestock.

Economic Innovation

Transforms raw alternative protein into a high-value bioactive ingredient with demonstrated functionality, opening new markets in functional foods.

Rigorous Validation

Scientific Evidence & Results

Protein hydrolysis converts large protein molecules into bioactive peptides. Our laboratory evaluations confirm significant improvements across all functional metrics.

Molecular Transformation

Laboratory evaluation via SDS-PAGE confirmed the successful conversion of large, complex cricket proteins into highly functional, low-molecular-weight peptide fractions (<10 kDa).

  • Safety: Eliminates large allergenic structures
  • Functionality: Dramatically improves aqueous solubility
  • Bioactivity: Unlocks hidden antioxidant properties

Hydrolysis successfully transformed cricket proteins into low-molecular-weight peptide fractions.
